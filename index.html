About Us

Yoga and Fitness Home - Pacification studio

Pacification Studio is Jaipur's premier yoga and fitness destination, nestled in the heart of Malviya Nagar near Airport Road. Under the expert guidance of Ajay Kumawat Sir, the studio offers a serene and welcoming environment where students of all levels can embark on a meaningful journey towards physical wellness and mental peace. With a perfect 5-star rating and a growing community of dedicated practitioners, Pacification Studio stands as a beacon of authentic yoga instruction in Rajasthan.

At Pacification Studio, we believe yoga is more than just exercise — it is a path to holistic well-being. Ajay Sir's profound knowledge of yoga asanas, his passion for teaching, and his ability to maintain high energy in every class make each session unique and deeply rewarding. Whether you are a beginner seeking flexibility or an experienced yogi looking to deepen your practice, our studio offers a safe, supportive, and blissful space for your daily yoga journey.

  • Perfect 5-Star Rating with 73 glowing reviews from satisfied students
  • Expert instructor Ajay Kumawat with deep, authentic knowledge of yoga asanas and teaching
  • Conveniently located near Jaipur Airport — easily accessible for locals and travelers alike
